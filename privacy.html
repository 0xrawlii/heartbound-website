Privacy First: Heartbound is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your character data, campaigns, and personal information stay on your device and are never uploaded to our servers without your explicit consent.

1. Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Your character sheets, campaign data, or game content
  • Personal gameplay information or statistics
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or personal files

Information We May Collect

  • Subscription Information: When you purchase a subscription, RevenueCat (our payment processor) collects necessary payment and subscription status information
  • Technical Information: Basic app performance and crash reporting data to improve the app
  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support, we keep records of our correspondence

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Provide customer support
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose not directly related to providing and improving the Heartbound app.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your game data (characters, campaigns, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or backup your data.

Cloud Services

We use these trusted third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For app distribution and basic analytics

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To our service providers (like RevenueCat) who help us operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we have about you
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your game data at any time
  • Cancel your subscription through the App Store or Google Play

6. Children's Privacy

Heartbound is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

7. International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that the information you provide will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers and service providers are located.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and on this website. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

App Stores

When you download Heartbound from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

support@heartbound.app

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all users of the Heartbound app.
